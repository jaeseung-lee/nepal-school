## Direct answer

A Korean employer planning an E-9 hire should confirm the eligible industry, workplace status and permitted headcount before recruitment. The general EPS sequence includes a required domestic recruitment effort, application to the competent employment center in the announced round, issuance of an employment permit, the standard labor contract, entry steps and continuing employment management.

## Procedure checklist

| Stage | Employer record |
| --- | --- |
| Eligibility | Industry, workplace, headcount, pay and hours |
| Domestic recruitment | Channel, period and consistent job conditions |
| Permit | Round, employment center, application and evidence |
| Contract/entry | Standard contract, insurance, education and arrival |
| Management | Wages, housing, safety, reports and workplace changes |

Build the target start date backward from the official permit round and the completion of contract, visa, entry and education steps. Explain duties, work location, pay components, deductions, housing and hours in an understood language. After entry, assign staff for arrival, orientation, safety, payroll questions and grievance handling. Keep reporting deadlines and grounds for any workplace change in the management calendar.

## FAQ

### Can the employer skip domestic recruitment?

The general EPS route normally requires the prescribed domestic recruitment effort. Confirm the current method and period for the applicable round.

### Can an employer freely nominate any candidate?

E-9 follows the EPS roster, referral and contract process. Review the [E-9 guide](/en/visa/e-9) and official notices before promising a placement.
