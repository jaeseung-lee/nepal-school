# KTS Catalog Sans font notice

`KtsCatalogSans-Regular.woff2` and `KtsCatalogSans-Bold.woff2` are format-converted,
renamed derivatives of Spoqa Han Sans Neo. The family and PostScript names were
changed so the derivative does not use the font's Reserved Font Names.

- Upstream: https://github.com/spoqa/spoqa-han-sans/tree/v3.3.0
- Upstream font revision: 1.100
- License: SIL Open Font License 1.1 (`KtsCatalogSans-OFL.txt`)
- Included weights: Regular 400 and Bold 700
- Coverage used here: Korean, Japanese, Latin, and shared CJK glyphs

The catalog generator converts these WOFF2 sources to temporary TTF files,
then ReportLab subsets and embeds them in the generated PDFs.
